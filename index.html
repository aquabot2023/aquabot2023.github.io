Problem

Ships encounter obstructions in narrow channels and under low bridges, causing delays and accidents. Damaged submarine cables disrupt critical communications networks.

Solution

AquaBot is a robotic system that helps ships navigate narrow channels and low bridges. It uses sensors and mapping technology to create real-time maps that allow captains to navigate easily.
